Famously known as the frontman of Queen, Farrokh Bulsara AKA Freddie Mercury, was born in 1946 in Stone Town, Zanzibar, and eventually rose as one of the most highly-regarded rock singers in history. Sadly, due to AIDS, he died in 1991 at the young age of 45.
